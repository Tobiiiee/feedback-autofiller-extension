# Privacy Policy for Feedback Auto-Filler

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2026

## Overview
Feedback Auto-Filler is a browser extension designed to help users quickly fill out feedback and evaluation forms. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
**QEC AutoFiller does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:
-  We do NOT collect any personal information
-  We do NOT track your browsing history
-  We do NOT send any data to external servers
-  We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
-  We do NOT sell or share any information

## Data Storage
The extension only stores:
-  Your last selected answer choice (A, B, C, or D)
-  Your preference for auto-filling comments

This data is stored **locally** in your browser using Chrome's built-in storage API and is never transmitted anywhere.

## Permissions
The extension requires the following permissions:

### activeTab
- **Purpose:** To access and modify the current tab's content when you click "Fill Form"
- **Usage:** Only activates when you explicitly click the extension button
- **Scope:** Limited to the currently active tab

### storage
- **Purpose:** To save your preferences locally in your browser
- **Usage:** Stores your selected options so you don't have to re-select them each time

### host_permissions (all_urls)
- **Purpose:** To work on any feedback form regardless of the URL
- **Usage:** Allows the extension to function on various survey and evaluation systems
- **Note:** The extension only interacts with pages when you explicitly activate it

## Third-Party Services
Feedback AutoFiller does NOT use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Changes to This Policy
If we make changes to this policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the extension developer through the Chrome Web Store.

## Open Source
This extension is open source. You can review the code to verify our privacy claims.

---

**In Summary:** Feedback Auto-Filler respects your privacy. It doesn't collect, track, or share anything. It simply helps you fill forms faster.
